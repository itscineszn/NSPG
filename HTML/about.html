About Us

Our Sweet Story

2016: The Beginning

What started as selling graham balls turned into something bigger. It all began with a small batch of graham balls, made with love and passion.

Trade Fair Milestone

We took a leap and joined our first trade fair to introduce our treats to more people. The experience was invaluable.

Business Growth

As word spread, our menu expanded. We started experimenting with different sweets like cakes in a tub, mousse, and other desserts.

Customer Appreciation

Through all the challenges, it’s the smiles on our customers' faces that keep us going. We’re grateful for the support and love we’ve received.

Looking Ahead

Together, we're not just making sweets — we're building dreams. With every creation, we're excited for the next chapter, and we're grateful to be growing side by side.
